Don’t believe the myths. There’s more to our midwives.

There are a number of myths about midwives, but have you heard all the facts? You may be missing out on the perfect provider for you. Did you know:

  • Certified nurse-midwives don’t just deliver babies. They provide health care to women during all stages of life, including annual well woman exams, screenings, prescribing medications, gynecologic care, and more.
  • Our midwives have a master’s or doctorate degree from an accredited nurse-midwife program, and have passed the American Midwifery Certification Board exam.
  • Our midwives deliver a health care experience tailored to you and can supply the spectrum of pain management options, including epidurals, nitrous oxide and hydrotherapy.
  • Research has shown that collaborative care practices -- with midwives working alongside physicians -- have fewer operative deliveries.
  • Midwives provide excellent health care along with the emotional support you often need when having a baby or experiencing other life changes.
