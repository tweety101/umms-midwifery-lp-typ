Are you ready for cold and flu season? At University of Maryland Community Medical Group — Primary Care, we are here to help you feel better fast. Our providers offer complete primary care services for you and your family, including:

  • Check-ups
  • Flu vaccinations
  • Health screenings
  • Treatment of short-term and long-term illnesses

In sickness and in health, we have you covered. If you do need us, our goal is to make your experience as easy and convenient as possible, so you can feel better fast. We offer:

  • Same-day appointments and extended hours available at some locations
  • Secure online access to your health record, test results and more
  • Email directly with your providers
  • Most insurance plans are accepted
