There’s more to our midwives. Experience the difference a midwife can make.

When you hear the word “midwife,” what comes to mind? A woman helping a mom-to-be deliver her baby? Well, that’s one definition of a midwife. But our midwives do so much more than deliver babies (although they are highly skilled at that too). They provide comprehensive care to women during all stages of life, from adolescence to their childbearing years and through menopause. Working side-by-side with our team of physicians, our midwives take the time to get to know you, so we can give you the personalized care you deserve.

Gynecologic Care

  • Annual wellness exams including pap smears, cancer screenings and healthy lifestyle counseling
  • Contraception counseling and management
  • Treatment of abnormal pap smears
  • STD screening and treatment

Gynecologic Care

  • Family planning
  • Non-invasive prenatal genetic testing
  • Childbirth including vaginal delivery and v-backs
  • Pain management options during delivery, including epidurals, nitrous oxide and hydrotherapy
  • Postpartum care
  • Lactation care and support

If you are planning a family, research has shown that collaborative care practices — midwives working alongside physicians — have fewer operative deliveries. This is just one reason why University of Maryland Community Medical Group – Women’s Health is proud to offer the services of highly skilled and trained certified nurse-midwives

We strive to make your experience as easy and convenient as possible:

  • Email about questions or concerns directly with your provider
